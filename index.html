FAQ

Questions I get
all the time.

How long does it take to build a Shopify store?
Most stores launch in 7 days. I use AI-enhanced workflows + a battle-tested process to deliver fast without cutting corners. Complex projects with custom functionality may take 2-3 weeks — I'll give you an honest timeline on our first call.
How much does a Shopify store cost?
Projects start at $500 for a clean store setup and go up based on complexity. Custom theme development, advanced integrations, and multi-currency setups are higher. I'll quote you a fixed price after understanding your needs — no hourly surprises.
Do you work with international clients?
Yes — I work with clients across India, the US, UK, Australia, Canada, and more. I communicate async via Loom videos, WhatsApp, and Slack. Time zones have never been an issue in 4+ years.
What if I need changes after launch?
Every project includes 7 days of free post-launch support. After that, I offer ongoing retainer plans or per-task pricing. Most of my clients come back for new features — I'm here for the long run, not a one-time transaction.
Can you redesign my existing store?
Absolutely. I redesign underperforming Shopify, WooCommerce, and Webflow stores regularly. I'll audit your current site, identify conversion blockers, and rebuild it to look and perform significantly better — while preserving your SEO and content.
Why should I hire you instead of an agency?
Agencies pass your project between juniors. With me, you get one senior developer — start to finish. Faster communication, no handoff delays, and I personally care about your store's success. That's why I have 100% job success on Upwork.
